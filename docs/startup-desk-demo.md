## Decision asked

> Should our tokenized startup allocate $10,000 of treasury to a 30-day TikTok organic marketing push targeting Solana developers?

**Context provided**:
- 2-person crypto startup, $200k treasury, idea coin launched
- MRR: $4k (mostly 1 enterprise pilot)
- Product: Solana-native prediction market analytics
- Target audience: Solana developers + PM traders
- Part-time creator, ~30hr/month
- Past channels: Twitter 2k organic in 6mo, TikTok untested
- Success metric: $10k spent → >$10k attributable MRR/pipeline within 90d

**Options**:
1. Allocate $10k to TikTok organic push
2. Allocate $10k to paid X ads instead
3. Split: $5k TikTok + $5k Discord sponsorships
4. Hold the $10k, defer marketing decision

**Reasoning**:

Discord sponsorships (Option 3 component) are a credible #2, but splitting budget with TikTok is capital-inefficient — concentrated X spend with an already-validated 2k organic following maximizes probability of the $10k → $10k MRR hurdle being cleared within 90 days.
